ABOUT US

We, the founders of Ekal Shiksha, have decided to incorporate individual classes for students. After five years of expertise in the education industry and dealing with the careers of almost 5000 students of K-12 segments, we became captivated by the fact that all the children are born with different capacities, capabilities, natures, mindsets, business-related and learning patterns.
We realised that the traditional group teaching method is not so effective for them in maintaining a broad, global perspective. After acknowledging the problems faced by children, we inferred that children need a platform where they can learn as per their way. Soon after that, we decided to launch our learning platform ‘Ekal Shiksha’ to provide one teacher to one student for their enrichment.
Our sole objective is to concentrate on the individual learning capacity of students and help them in enhancing their skills after a deep comprehension of their unique as well as individual learning needs. At Ekal Shiksha, we let young minds study at their own pace. We have accommodated well-trained and proficient mentors from all over the country who can lay the flexibility of choosing and designing the lesson plans and learning methods for each student. It will prove beneficial as students can learn without fearing the judgement of teachers and their batchmates. Along with that, we also have our doubt rectification team that be available next to students 24*7 at a very accessible fee structure.

The name of our platform, Ekal Shiksha, itself defines its purpose. Ekal ~ Individual and Shiksha ~ Education.

Our features

  • Free demo individual classes

  • LIVE audio-video linking between the teacher and the student

  • Well designed individual classes as per time table to maintain regularity

  • Recorded live lessons for revision

  • Immediate Doubt resolution

  • The feedback system for students and parents based on academic performance

  • Weekly tests

  • Mock tests and monthly progress report with improvement classes.

Logo

Ekal Shiksha is an Ed-tech company which provides one on one individual online classes. It is an initiative which aims to revolutionize the standard teaching and learning methods. We believe that each student has his or her personal learning speed, method and curriculum which is very important for a teacher to understand and personalize their teaching methods according to the students not vice versa. This platform is especially built for those who have immense potential but are never focused on in group classes, the growth of such students is our moto.
We have been in the teaching industry for past four years, catered to more than 1000s of students till the date. We are unique in a manner that we not only provide tuitions but also we make sure that the performance of each student is tracked through our LMS system and they get to check their clearity of concepts by the MCQs provided after each concept is taught. We have adopted the EAD approach for the better learning, understanding and self assessing of students. We are in a way parent friendly and make sure that the parents are kept in loop for each activity of the student and the teacher.
We have accommodated the best of talented teachers from all across the India for students who believe in learning beyond a classroom. We are a one step solution for all those parents who worry about their kid not being focused enough in group classes, for those who believe that their child can do far better if focused a little more, for those who worry for their kid’s safety when they are at work and their child alone with a teacher at home, for those who worry sending their child to coaching at odd hours and most of all for those who not only believe in passing but in learning.
